A bit about me

I was born and raised in Fort Collins, CO by two incredible parents. Life there is nice and sweet, but I eventually moved to Denver seeking a more fast-paced environment.

Since I was a wee tot, I've been interested in technology. I grew up using devices- from Nintendo systems to the old Nokia brick phones to computers. There were incredible technological strides in the 90's and 2000's, so I naturally grew up using the internet. I remember using Netscape Navigator and Encyclopedia Britannica and AskJeeves, you know, cool kid stuff. I have always wanted to take part in building on the web, and now I can

I recently graduated from General Assembly's Web Development Immersive program. My world was rocked by this experience but I learned a great deal and I am now continuing my learning by building pet projects and games.
